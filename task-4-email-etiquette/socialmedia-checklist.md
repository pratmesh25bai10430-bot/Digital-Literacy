### Part-B: Social Media Responsibility Checklist

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Review and update your privacy controls often to stay aware of your audience. | Avoid sharing sensitive details such as IDs, addresses, or restricted academic work. |
| Maintain a polished profile and concise description on platforms like LinkedIn. | Refrain from posting emotional rants or negative remarks about faculty or peers online. |
| Cross-check facts and sources before reposting or forwarding any content. | Do not use others’ work without credit or distribute protected content unlawfully. |
| Build meaningful connections with professionals and alumni in your field of interest. | Stay away from uploading or promoting content that breaks laws or institutional rules. |
| Be mindful when tagging people; ensure their consent and comfort. | Never rely on deletion as a guarantee that content is completely erased. |
